Natty

Natty is the collaborative work of Cat McKay and Fiona Gibb.Besides their beautiful handmade Rimu jewellery range, Natty also design and produce hand-printed textiles, illustrated gift cards and prints. The latest range celebrates Aotearoa, and includes a beautiful selection of tea towels and pillowcases.

The team at Natty try to evoke and celebrate the natural environment with their product, rather than impact on it.

Robyn Reyolds

Robyn Reynolds has been designing and making unique women’s clothing for over twenty years.

Her love of interesting, colourful and textured fabric led her into the world of fashion design. The popularity of her Aline Eva Skirt is testament to her talent and art form.

Ella Made Me

Ellamademe is a product design partnership specialising in high quality and unique etched wooden accessories.

Selling a range of bamboo phone cases and contemporary jewellery inspired by the bush and beaches of the West Auckland, NZ. They believe in using natural, recyclable materials as much as possible and making items you can be proud to own and that the planet will thank you for.
